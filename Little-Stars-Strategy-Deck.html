Executive Summary
The Path to 120 Students

Little Stars Academy can increase enrollment by fixing three core issues: weak visibility, poor reputation, and poor lead tracking. The plan is to reach 120 students by September and avoid a summer drop by rolling out a rebrand (new site, updated profiles), running Google and Meta ads, expanding into key directories, using physical marketing and consistently using referrals and events to drive demand. At the same time, implementing a CRM and standardizing follow-up will improve conversion, with targets of 20 tours per month and a 50% tour-to-enrollment rate, supported by stronger reviews (50+ at 4.5 stars) and top-three search rankings for key terms.
